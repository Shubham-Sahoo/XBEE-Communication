\section{Mathematical Model}


r $\rightarrow$ Radius of Wheels\newline
d $\rightarrow$Distance Between Wheels\newline
R $\rightarrow$ Radius of turn\newline
$\omega$ $\rightarrow$Angular Velocity of car\newline
$\omega_L$ $\rightarrow$Angular Velocity of left Wheel\newline
$\omega_R$ $\rightarrow$Angular Velocity of Right Wheel\newline

Now,\newline
    $V_R = \omega (R+d)$ \newline
    $V_L = \omega R$ \newline
    $\omega_R = \omega(R+d)/r$  \newline
     $\omega_L = \omega(R)/r$ \newline
    $\omega_R = \omega_L + \omega(d/r)$ $\rightarrow$ Equation 1 \newline 
    \newline
    \newline
    From IMU Readings we $g_L$ and $g_R$ \newline
    
$g_L = \sqrt{\omega_L^2 + \omega^2}$ $\rightarrow$ Equation 2 \newline
$g_R = \sqrt{\omega_R^2 + \omega^2}$ $\rightarrow$ Equation 3  \newline

Now by using equation 1,equation 2 and equation 3, We can find out $\omega_R$, $\omega_L$,$\omega$
